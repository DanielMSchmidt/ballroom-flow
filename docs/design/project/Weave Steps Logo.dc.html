Weave Steps — brand mark

the "woven W" · adopted 2026-07-05 · chosen from three candidates (woven W / step marks / rhythm weave)

The mark & the idea

Two dancers' paths cross and interlock into a W. The right strand breaks where the left passes over it — the literal weave of the name, and the over-under of a partnered figure. One geometry, one weight (stroke 2.4 in a 24-unit box, round caps), studio blue #2f5d8f on warm paper #e9e6df — or paper strokes on a blue tile when it needs its own ground.
